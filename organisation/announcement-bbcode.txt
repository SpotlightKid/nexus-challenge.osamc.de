Subject: The Open Source Music Nexus Challenge starts September 19th, 2019

From the organizers of the wonderful [url=https://fmchallenge.osamc.de]FM Challenge of 2018[/url], [url=https://osamc.de]Open Source Audio Meeting Cologne[/url] proudly presents:

[b][size=150][color=#800000]The Open Source Music Nexus Challenge[/color][/size][/b]

[url=https://nexus-challenge.osamc.de/][attachment=0]nexus-challenge-banner-600.jpg[/attachment][/url]
[color=#404080][url]https://nexus-challenge.osamc.de/[/url][/color]

[b]What:[/b]

[list=1]
[*] Make a music track:
[list=a]
[*] [i]"in-the-style-of"[/i] a well-known artist or song of your choosing,
[*] using [i]a recording of a physical sound source[/i], made by yourself,
[*] using [i]only [url=https://www.gnu.org/philosophy/free-sw.html]Open Source software[/url][/i] (exception: proprietary DAWs).[/list]
[*] [url=https://archive.org/create/]Upload[/url] it to archive.org under a Creative Commons license
[*] [url=https://nexus-challenge.osamc.de/submit/]Submit[/url] it into the competition.[/list]

Before you enter the competition, be sure to read the [url=https://nexus-challenge.osamc.de/rules/]detailed rules[/url]!

[b]When:[/b]

The competition starts on [b]September 19th, 2019[/b] and runs for one month until the end of [b]October 18th, 2019[/b].

[b]Who:[/b]

[b]Everybody[/b] (i.e. every natural person) can enter the competition.

The tools to use are Free Software and getting started with [url=https://libremusicproduction.com/]music production using Open Source software[/url] is easy and free to everyone!

[b]Winning:[/b]

The winner of the competition is selected by [url=https://nexus-challenge.osamc.de/vote/]general vote[/url].

Voting starts on [b]October 19th, 2019[/b] and is open for [b]seven days[/b].

The entry receiving the most points wins and will be presented at the third [url=https://sonoj.org/]Sonoj Convention[/url], which takes place on the weekend of [b]October 26th / 27th[/b] in Cologne.

Second and third places will be awarded as well. We do not give out physical prizes, but the winners will receive fame and glory :) and the community as a whole also wins!

---

Please share this announcement with all interested parties. If you wish to sponsor prizes for the competition winners, please contact the organizer!
